Virtual Classes

What are they?

We are holding small classes with 1-2 people learning to knit. These are our first classes, so we are experimenting and testing with them a little bit.

How long are the classes?

The classes are around 1-1.5 hours each, and it’ll take an additional 2 hours outside of the classes to complete the project.

Where are they?

The pilot classes will be held online on Google Meet.

What happens to what we knit?

Once it is safe to drop off the rectangles, you will have the option of giving a CFC member you know the rectangles or dropping them off in drop-off boxes that we will set up when it is safe.

Materials

You will need the following materials to participate in the pilot classes:

  • At least 40-45 yards of worsted weight yarn (makes one rectangle)
    • Acrylic is preferred, but any fiber works!
  • 1 pair of knitting needles (4.5 - 5.5 mm/ US 7-9) Just one pair is fine!
    • Straight single pointed needles
  • 1 Yarn needle (these are NOT knitting needles!)
    • If you don’t have a yarn needle, a bobby pin or crochet hook will work!
