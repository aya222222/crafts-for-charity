Materials

You will need the following materials to participate in the pilot classes:

  • At least 40-45 yards of worsted weight yarn (makes one rectangle)
  • 1 pair of knitting needles (4.5 - 5.5 mm/ US 7-9) Just one pair is fine!
  • 1 Yarn needle (these are NOT knitting needles!)
