VIRTUAL CLASSES

What are they?

We are holding classes with 1-2 people per teacher of learning how to knit. However, classes of 3 or less are preferred.

How long are the classes?

The classes are usually 45-60 minutes long, and to completely knit the rectangles, it will take around 3 hours.

Where are they?

They will be online on Google Meet. We will send you the information once you sign up.

What happens to what we knit?

You will have two options to give us your rectangle(s). You can either give them to one of our CFC members, or drop them off in a drop-off box that we will set up. We will use the rectangles to create various different items for charities.
