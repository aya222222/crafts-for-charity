DONATE CRAFTS

Currently, we are accepting knitted or crocheted 7 x 9 inch rectangles as well as knitted or crocheted 6 x 60 inch scarves. We currently have a drop off box located at Happy Helpers for the Homeless (1550 Cartons Center Drive Halethorpe, MD 21227). Further requirements are listed below.

  • Measurements: Must be as close to 7 x 9 inches as possible. We will accept rectangles off by no more than 2 inches on either side.
  • Yarn: Must be machine washable and dryable. Medium worsted-weight yarn is preferable, however, we will accept rectangles made of different thicknesses.
  • Measurements: Must be as close to 6 x 60 inches as possible. We will accept scarves off by no more than .5 inches wide and 3 inches long.
  • Yarn: Must be machine washable and dryable. Chunky or Bulky weight yarn is preferable, however, we will accept scarves made of different thicknesses.
