DONATE MATERIALS

Crafts for Charity is currently accepting the materials listed below. We currently have a drop off box located at Happy Helpers for the Homeless (1550 Cartons Center Drive Halethorpe, MD 21227).

Materials Accepted

  • Crochet hooks of any size (preferably 5.5 - 6.5 mm)
  • Single pointed knitting needles of any size (preferably 4.5 - 5.5 mm)
  • Yarn
    • Must be machine washable and dryable
    • Preferably worsted weight or bulkier
  • Knitting/Crochet Accessories
    • Stitch markers
    • Yarn needles
    • Any other accessories
