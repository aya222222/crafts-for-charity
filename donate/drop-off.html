Drop Off Crafts or Donated Supplies

Drop Off Boxes

We currently have a drop off box located at Happy Helpers for the Homeless (1550 Catons Center Drive Halethorpe, MD 21227).

Steps to Drop Off

First, take all of your crafts or donated materials, and put them in a Ziploc or plastic grocery bag. Make sure that they are as closed off to the environment as possible.

Next, fill out, submit, and print the form below with what you are bringing. If you do not have ability to print the form, a piece of paper with the information will work. Make sure the form is inside your bag before you drop it off, and you are all set!
