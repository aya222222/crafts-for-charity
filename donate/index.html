Where do your donations go?

Any donations will go directly to provide materials and tools for classes. They will help us purchase materials for classes such as yarn, knitting needles, crochet hooks, tripods, and other materials to help the teachers and students. This way our classes can work more efficiently and we can work better to help our community.

It’s tax-deductible!

Crafts for Charity is fiscally sponsored by Robotics for All, Inc, a 501c(3) organization, so all donations are tax-deductible. (This is why the donation is first going to Robotics for All, but all your donations will be transferred to Crafts for Charity.)
