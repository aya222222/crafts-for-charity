What is the timeline for the application process?

We will be accepting applications for each season a month prior to the start of each season and up to 1 week before the start of each season.

  • Summer Applications: June 1st - June 24th
  • Fall Applications: August 15th - September 8th
  • Winter Applications: November 11th - December 4th
  • Spring Applications: February 12th - March 5th

What are the dates for each season?

The dates for each season are as follows:

  • Summer: 7/1 - 9/1, 9 weeks in total
  • Fall: 9/15 - 11/20, 11/30 - 12/4, 10 weeks in total
  • Winter: 12/11 - 12/23, 1/2 - 1/25, 2/1 - 3/5, 10.5 weeks in total
  • Spring: 3/12 - 4/1, 4/7 - 6/1, 11 weeks in total

Are there any dues I need to pay to join?

No, we do not require anyone to pay dues, however, we appreciate all donations of money and/or craft supplies! For teaching members, currently, we require you to use your own yarn and craft supplies because we do not have a safe way to distribute materials at the moment. When it is safer, we will try to give materials to anyone who needs them for free.
