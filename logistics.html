LOGISTICS

Logistics team members are responsible for legal and technological work for Crafts for Charity including researching and preparing class logistics. Tasks may differ on a week by week basis and will be assigned during weekly online meetings.

Time Commitment

Each week, Crafts for Charity Logistics members are expected to attend one Logistics team call as well as one full CFC group call. Both calls will last between 30 minutes to 1 hour. Tasks will be assigned during these calls and weekly tasks will take between 30 minutes to 3 hours. CFC operates on a season by season basis and members are expected to stay through full seasons. The current summer season lasts from July 1st to September 1st.

Benefits

  • Help your community and local charities
  • Earn service hours for all work completed for Crafts for Charity
  • Build stronger researching, problem solving, and analytical skills as well as gain experience working with teams within an organization
  • Gain valuable experience that can be used on resumes for future jobs, internships, or college applications

Requirements

  • Aged 14+
    • Exceptions may be made to highly qualified 13 year olds
  • Strong technical and problem solving skills
  • Ability to work on Crafts for Charity tasks for at least 2 hours a week

Desirable Qualifications

  • Previous volunteer experience
  • Familiarity with solving technical issues in call platforms like Zoom and Google Meet
  • Digital organization skills with documents, files, etc.
