WHY DID I START CFC?

I have always had a love for arts and crafts and wanted to use my passion to help others. I started Crafts for Charity to be able to spread my love for crafting, allowing everyone at any age or skill level to volunteer their time in a fun and creative way to create crafts that will be used to better the world! I believe in the importance of opening opportunities to everyone in the arts and volunteering fields and I want our free and accessible craft classes to achieve our mission of spreading creativity while helping others!
