WHY DID WE START CFC?

We have both always had a love for arts and crafts and wanted to use our passion to help those in need. We started Crafts for Charity to be able to spread our love for crafting, allowing everyone at any age or skill level to volunteer their time in a fun and creative way to create crafts that will be used to better the world! We believe in the importance of opening opportunities to everyone in the arts and volunteering fields and we want our free and accessible craft classes to achieve our mission of spreading creativity while helping others!
