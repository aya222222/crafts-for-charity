MEMBER POSITIONS

Members of Crafts for Charity are divided into four teams: Logistics, Outreach, Teaching, and Management. Each team has different tasks and responsibilities and members in each team work together to complete these tasks and responsibilities. Volunteers are essential to Crafts for Charity and we appreciate each and every volunteer that works with us. If you are interested in volunteering, please read through position descriptions then fill out an application here:

Logistics

Do legal and technological work including researching, preparing class logistics, etc.

Outreach

Find organizations that we can partner with, post on social media to promote Crafts for Charity, and update our website and other social media platforms as necessary.

Teaching

Teach students the crafts (currently knitting), make video tutorials on how to do the crafts, and record an inventory of donated crafts.
