TEACHING

Teaching team members are responsible for teaching students crafts (currently knitting), making video tutorials on how to do the crafts, and recording an inventory of donated crafts. Tasks may differ on a week by week basis and will be assigned during weekly online meetings.

Time Commitment

Each week, Crafts for Charity Teaching members are expected to attend one Teaching team call as well as one full CFC group call. Both calls will last between 30 minutes to 1 hour. Tasks will be assigned during these calls and weekly tasks will take between 30 minutes to 3 hours. CFC operates on a season by season basis and members are expected to stay through full seasons. The current summer season lasts from July 1st to September 1st.

Benefits

  • Help your community and local charities
  • Earn service hours for all work completed for Crafts for Charity
  • Build stronger interpersonal, problem solving, and leadership skills as well as gain experience working with teams within an organization
  • Positions on the management team may be offered in the future
  • Gain valuable experience that can be used on resumes for future jobs, internships, or college applications

Requirements

  • Aged 14+
    • Exceptions may be made to highly qualified 13 year olds
  • Familiarity with at least one of the crafts being taught (you may learn from a CFC class if you do not have previous experience)
  • Ability to work on Crafts for Charity tasks for at least 2 hours a week

Desirable Qualifications

  • Previous volunteer experience
  • Has taken Crafts for Charity class(es)
  • Experience with teaching crafts to any age group
  • Experience with filming videos
