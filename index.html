OUR MISSION

Crafts for Charity’s mission is to create enjoyable opportunities for those who want to volunteer creatively to help their community. We do this by providing free virtual craft classes that teach the skills needed to donate crafts to those in need.

LEARN

We teach knitting in our online classes!

CREATE

You make items like knitted rectangles and scarves!

DONATE

We donate your creations to charities!
