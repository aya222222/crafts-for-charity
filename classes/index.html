VIRTUAL CLASSES

What are they?

We are holding knitting classes for 1-3 students per class led by two teachers. These classes will teach you how to knit a rectangle to help people in need!

How long are the classes?

Classes are usually 45-90 minutes long, and to finish knitting the rectangles, it will take around 2 extra hours outside of the class!

Where are they?

Classes will be held online on video call platforms like Google Meet, Zoom, Facetime, or Instagram. We will send you more information once you sign up.

What happens to what we knit?

You will have two options to give us your rectangle(s). You can either give them to one of our CFC members or drop them off in a drop-off box. We currently have a drop off box located at Happy Helpers for the Homeless (1550 Cartons Center Drive Halethorpe, MD 21227). We will use the rectangles to create blankets that will be donated to Happy Helpers for the Homeless!
